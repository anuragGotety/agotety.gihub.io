Portal Defender

Defend a malfunctioning portal from intergalactic invaders. Be careful not to get too close or you’ll get sucked into your doom!
Designer and Gameplay Programmer

  • This was a solo project, built from scratch using public domain assets.
  • The core mechanics were created using Euler calculations.
  • I had also created animations being triggered by registering collisions.
  • Randomly spawned enemies and powerups in different locations of the map.
  • UI system displaying player information was also created.
