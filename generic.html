A collection of my films

As a filmmaker I really enjoy cinematography and writing stories. Directing and bringing those stories to life gives me the utmost joy.

Overtime and Overdue

Writer. Director. Editor
Sam, a scatterbrained twenty-something plays videogames while her girlfriend, Ishita, waits for her at the Sample Gates. She arrives late for the date and tries various ways to amuse Ishita. However, when Sam pops the question for absolute commitment, will Ishita accept or learn that some people may never change?

The Waiting Game

Writer. Director. Editor
Patience is a virtue very few possess.
Follow a man as he eagerly awaits his Dominos pizza to arrive. He indulges in all kinds of vices in order to speed up time.

The Suspicion

Writer. Director. Editor
In this short, a girl comes home after a long day. She has a feeling that her boyfriend isn't being faithful to her. Do her suspicions get confirmed after a search of his room?

A Weekend In San Francisco

Director. Editor
A Chronicle of two friends exploring the various neighborhoods of San Francisco. Their trip takes them to the artsy districts as well as the financial sector. Discover the Bay Area through the lens of two young travelers in this homage to the West Coast.
