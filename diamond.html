Diamond Dash:

Worked in a team of four, built in godot. Worked on enemy spawning via procedural generation and core gameplay loop. Also did the UI which included countdown mechanics and player information.

Details

  • Implemented the player life system as well as attack mechanism.
  • Scripted two different types of enemies, one with a patrol circuit and another that follows the player.
  • Implemented animations which are triggered when enemies are attacked.
  • Created a UI bar that displays score, lives remaining and countdown to destruction.
